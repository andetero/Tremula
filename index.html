What Tremula monitors

Everything happening
on and around Earth

Seven real-time data streams, all free, all government-maintained, delivered to your pocket the moment something worth knowing happens.

🌊

Earthquakes

Real-time USGS seismic data. See M4.5+ events globally and M2.5+ near you, with magnitude, depth, and location — within minutes of occurring.

✨

Aurora Borealis

NOAA Kp index updated every 3 hours. Know when the northern lights might be visible from your latitude tonight — from quiet to extreme storm.

🛸

ISS Tracking

Real-time International Space Station location. See exactly where it is right now and how far from you — with context about what region it's flying over.

☄️

Asteroids & Meteors

NASA close approach data for the day, plus upcoming meteor shower alerts with peak rates and viewing tips — loaded even without a connection.

🌋

Volcanic Activity

USGS volcanic explosion detections logged alongside seismic data. The same network that monitors earthquakes catches volcanic events too.

📡

Offline Support

Last known data is cached locally. When you're off the grid, Tremula shows what it last saw with a clear timestamp — you're never left with a blank screen.
